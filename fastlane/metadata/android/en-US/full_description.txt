Places - Address Book is a simple Application to store and manage addresses of your favorite places and contacts.

Highlights of the App:
1. Save Addresses and link contacts to those Places.
2. Navigate to the saved Addresses of Places.
3. Assign a category like Friends, Family, Professional etc for the places/addresses.
4. Complete address of the places you visit.
5. Easy UI to select the location/address using GPS.
6. Landmarks of the places/address.
7. Quick action links of addresses to google map.
8. Call, Message, Mail actions are available for the contacts linked to places/addresses
9. Copy and share google map link of the Places/addresses
9. Copy and share address.

All these features available for FREE and NO ADS !!!
